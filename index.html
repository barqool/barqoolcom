Barqool

We're based in Dundee, Scotland, and we’re on a bold mission to tackle household food waste. Barqool helps you manage shared groceries, discover smart recipes, and track expenses — all in one powerful, community-first app.


We're not just building tools for consumers — Barqool is also exploring partnerships with businesses and organizations to help them monitor inventory, improve efficiency, and reduce avoidable food waste across their operations.

Why It Matters

10.7 Million Tonnes

Food waste generated annually in the UK — most of it from households.

£21.8 Billion

The total value of edible food wasted. Let’s redirect that to better use.

Local Impact

In Scotland alone, over 1 million tonnes of food go to waste every year. Barqool was born here to fight that.

Barqool is proudly built in Dundee, Scotland — a city with a heart for sustainability and community-driven innovation.
